About BD Electric

BDElectric is ready to service all of your electrical needs! We work with both residential and commercial clients throughout the entire Kansas City metropolitan area. We also work with a number of general contractors on commercial projects that start from the ground up, tenant finishes, remodels, fire/water damage restorations and residential new construction.

Our team is ready and happy to help you - - whether you simply need an outlet change or if you have your eye on some land and are ready to break ground on the project of your dreams! Our electricians are professional, knowledgeable and take pride in a job well done. They are trained and experienced to troubleshoot and fix any electrical problem that you may have in your home or business.

Our team wants you to be pleased with the end result and feel comfortable with the entire process. You will be treated with fairly and with respect. We offer high quality work at a competitive price.

BDElectric is licensed and insured.
